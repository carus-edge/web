Government Funding

The government wants to pay for your AI upgrade

Multiple federal and provincial programs are actively funding AI adoption for Atlantic Canadian businesses right now. We help you identify, apply for, and use this funding. Here are just a few:

ACOA RAII
Regional Artificial Intelligence Initiative funding specifically for Atlantic Canadian businesses adopting AI. Covers up to 75% of eligible costs.

Non-repayable funding for businesses impacted by U.S. tariffs. Covers automation, digitization, and technology integration to boost competitiveness.

Regional Economic Growth through Innovation covers up to 75% of eligible costs for technology adoption, productivity improvement, and scaling.
